# Personal Conventions

The author's personal coding style and conventions for this project. Apply them
whenever writing, reviewing, or refactoring code. This is a living document,
updated as preferences are discovered or refined during development.

## Precedence

1. **Existing codebase patterns win.** If a file, sibling, or established
   pattern already exists, follow it. Consistency beats personal preference.
2. **These conventions are the author's defaults** for when the codebase is
   silent or a new pattern is being established.
3. **Framework/product guidelines still apply** for domain-specific rules
   (Laravel, Inertia, Pest, Tailwind, etc.). These conventions layer personal
   style on top and do not override their technical guidance.
